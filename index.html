About avasky

Our service enables your business ( or personal project ) with a decentralized verification and login mechanism via MagicLinks. Be it access to company resources or a way to verify skill, our platform makes it easy to extend and customize. As an example, we created an add-on called Skydrop, allowing for quick content sharing via skylinks.

We kept away the clutter from the UI to deliver a straightforward experience for your users, as well as your business. MagicLinks only require an email to get verified and logged in!
